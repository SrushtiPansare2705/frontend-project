Football

Football, also called soccer, is played between two teams of eleven players each. Players score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent’s net. The game is popular worldwide and requires speed, strategy, teamwork, and endurance. It is governed internationally by FIFA.

Basic Rules of Football

  • Each team has 11 players, including a goalkeeper.
  • The match is played in two halves of 45 minutes each.
  • Goals are scored by getting the ball into the opponent’s net.
  • No hands are allowed except by the goalkeeper inside the penalty area.
  • Fouls result in free kicks or penalty kicks.
  • The team with more goals wins the match.

Benefits of Playing Football

  • Improves stamina and physical fitness
  • Enhances teamwork and communication skills
  • Boosts concentration and strategic thinking
  • Promotes mental and physical health
