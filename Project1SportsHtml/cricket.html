Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each. Teams take turns to bat and bowl. It is very popular in India, England, Australia, and Pakistan. The game requires skill, strategy, and teamwork.

Basic Rules of Cricket

  • Each team has 11 players.
  • The bowler delivers the ball to the batsman.
  • The batsman scores runs by hitting the ball.
  • Fielders try to get the batsman out.
  • The team with the highest runs wins.
  • Cricket has different formats: Test, ODI, and T20.

Famous Cricket Players

  • Sachin Tendulkar
  • MS Dhoni
  • Virat Kohli
  • Rohit Sharma
  • Rahul Dravid

Benefits of Playing Cricket

  • Improves fitness and stamina
  • Enhances focus and concentration
  • Builds teamwork and leadership skills
  • Teaches strategy and discipline
