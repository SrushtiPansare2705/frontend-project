Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced game played between two teams of five players each. Players score points by shooting the ball into the opponent’s basket. The game requires teamwork, speed, and good coordination. It was invented by James Naismith.

Basic Rules of Basketball

  • Each team has 5 players on the court.
  • Dribbling is required while moving with the ball.
  • Points are scored by shooting into the basket.
  • Two or three points are awarded for a basket.
  • The game is divided into four quarters.
  • Fouls are given for unfair physical contact.

Famous Basketball Players

  • Michael Jordan
  • LeBron James
  • Kobe Bryant
  • Stephen Curry

Benefits of Playing Basketball

  • Improves fitness and stamina
  • Builds teamwork and leadership skills
  • Develops concentration and discipline
  • Keeps body active and healthy
